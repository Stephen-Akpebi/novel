NOVEL RESIDENTIAL LIFE

As part of our commitment to create a conducive learning environment that provides holistic education, Novel Academy offers a ‘‘Home from Home’’ boarding facility. Aves Boarding is open to learners at the Cambridge Secondary stage (Year 7+).

Residents will benefit from heightened interaction with other learners, build discipline & responsibility in a safe & secure environment, whilst developing skills needed for higher education and for life.
