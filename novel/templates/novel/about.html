Welcome to Novel Academy

Welcome to the Novel Academy website which provides a snapshot of what Novel Academy is all about. It will also keep you informed about our busy, and exciting academic calendar and program.

Novel Academy is a family school, which aims to be the international school of choice. As a hybrid school, In Novel Academy our programs are designed to provide students with the mastery of the fundamental concepts necessary for achievement in each academic subject area. Novel Academy offers the GES and the British curriculum Programme for preschool to Grades School in a residential school environment at the Campus Adjacent AdomCity Estate Community 26. MISSION

To champion a new way of learning that prepares minds, transform hearts and develops the spirit of inquiry in all our students as basic tool for encouraging critical thinking and powering hands to impact the society.

VISION

To build a tradition of excellence and Godliness in our students, that integrates academic achievements and principles of integrity to enhance the transformation of Ghana, Africa and the world at large.

CLASSES

We have a small classroom concept with a maximum intake of 15 learners per class. An enabling environment is created for our Novatores to extend their existing skills and develop confidence through computer training, developing reading and writing skills, knowledge of basic mathematics, and provoke the power of imagination and innovation. The broad curricula for these units are centered on the following guidelines: - Communication, Language proficiency and Literacy - Personal, Social and Emotional Development - Physical Development - Creativity and innovation - Mathematics and critical thinking -Knowledge and Understanding of the World
